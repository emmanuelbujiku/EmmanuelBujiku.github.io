About Me

I am Emmanuel, a dedicated Data Analyst and Business Intelligence(BI) Analyst passionate about working with organisations that want to make effective use of their data; from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable the organisation in making evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences. I have also worked in some of personal projects making me comfortable in the use of analytical tools such as SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Python and Excel.

Education

Graduated from the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania (2019 - 2022) with Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. Throughout my Data Analytics learning journey I have managed to complete an extensive 6-month Google Data analytics Professional certificate Training and a 3-month advanced Google Business Intelligence Certificate Training. Both of these programs have impacted me with great practical knowledge applicable in real-world scenarios of Business Intelligence and Data Analytics.
